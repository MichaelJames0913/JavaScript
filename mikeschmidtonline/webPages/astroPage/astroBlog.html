Source: constellation-guide.com

Source: earthsky.org

The Andromeda Galaxy could be seen earlier in the evening on left side of image, the Andromeda galaxy is the most distant thing you can see with the naked eye; it's the closeset galaxy to our milky way at approximately 2.5 million light years from earth.
